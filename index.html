MYSELF

Greetings! My name is Rosie Ellis I'm a passionate UI and UX designer who is also a web developer from New York City. My experience has taught me that good design is not created in a moment of inspiration or a bubble. It is an iterative process requiring collabaration between multiple teams and your end users. It is easy to get attached to initial design solutions and forgo research and testing but this rarely works. First solutions are often wrong because in the early stages it is hard to know enough about the proble you are attempting to solve.
